Our Vision:
Successful Business

Within India lies the possibility for great development and enormous infrastructure demand. For many years, Japan has been a development partner to India and has been cooperating with India by providing financial support on a global scale. To push forward sustainable growth, Japan supports a variety of Indian initiatives like "Make in India", "Skill India", and "Clean India".

Industrial / Corporate Set up India, the land of opportunities is attracting Japanese investors and businesses to set up operations in India and has a fair share in one of the world's fastest-growing economies.

"The Indian economy is the world's tenth-largest by nominal GDP and third-largest by purchasing power parity (PPP).
